Who We Are

Excellence in Dubai Passenger Transport Since 2020

GreenStone Passengers Transport By Rented Buses LLC has served schools, colleges, hotels, site workers, and businesses with safe, professional bus charter services. Our corporate clients include contracting, engineering, trading, mechanical, and educational sectors. We combine an extensive fleet with flexible leasing options—including exclusive rebates for long-term corporate partnerships—so your transport needs are covered end to end.

Since 2020, we have focused on punctuality, well-maintained vehicles, and trained drivers and support staff. Whether you need daily shuttles, event transport, or tourism services, we aim to be your first choice in the UAE.
